How people in Canada get the new iPhone 11 for just 2.80$

100.325 Views
30.753 Likes
Published: 2 days ago

Publicerad: Henrik Bengtsson


If you live in Canada and you want the brand new iPhone 11, this could be the most electrifying article you’ve ever read!

This is the deal: Apple Inc (NASDAQ: AAPL) offers the new iPhone 11 for 2.80$. Yes, you’ve read it correctly, 2.80$.

As part of a special promotion, iPhone cooperates with Organizejobs, the reliable delivery partner, to offer iPhone 11 with a 99% discount on the normal price.

Why offer this crazy promotion? The marketing accountable Joel Branson answers: "Last year, Apple market share in Canada decreased by 35% and now it’s losing against Android worldwide. Apple wants to recover customers offering iPhones at an extremely low price to convert them in faithful clients, sharing the message with their friends "

Too good to be true? That’s what Carl Henderson, 18, thought as well. He decided to try and got his iPhone 11 for 2.80$ some days later.

Carl explains: \"At the beginning I thought it was just another Internet fraud, but then I had some research and saw that Organizejobs is a legitimate marketing organisation, so I decided to risk my 2.80$. After all, it’s always less than a meal price! 3 days later, anyway, I check my mail and I’m enthusiastic to see a new shiny box with the iPhone logo on it...".

Appearantly, this \"practically free \" promotional technique is a common practice among big firms with big marketing budgets. For example, Burger King launched a similar campaign in 2013, offering 20,000 free Whoopers on Facebook.

How to reclaim your iPhone 11 for 2.80$:

1. Just visit the iPhone 11 2.80$ official promotion page here

2. Insert your e-mail address and pay 2.80$

During this special promotion, you’ll also receive a FREE wireless charger (if it’s still available). Your product will be sent immediately (shipping costs included) and you’ll receive it in 3-5 days.

Click here to verify if iPhone 11 2.80$ is still available

So, you’ll be able to get an iPhone 11 for an extremely convenient price. But is the new iPhone 11 really the best smartphone on the market? ?

Our Revision Department compared iPhone 11 to many other telephones and this is what we found out: compared to previous versions of iOS, iPhone 11 is number 1 considering call quality, battery battery life, screen resolution, message speed and delivery.

Shortly, this telephone is the best and most reliable on the market. Also consider that it can be bought for only 2.80$, choice can be easily done.

Update (): In order to verify if Organizejobs is solid, our editorial team paid 2.80$ for an iPhone 11 last weekend and we actually received it last morning!
